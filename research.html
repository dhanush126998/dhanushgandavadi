RESEARCH

Research interests and current projects

Programmable biomaterials and molecular systems for understanding and controlling cancer behavior.

01

3D tumor invasion platforms

A tunable dual-hydrogel platform for modeling tumor invasion, quantifying radial migration, isolating invaded and non-invaded populations, and evaluating drug response in cancer cell lines and patient-derived models.

02

Synthetic extracellular matrices

Fully defined matrices engineered to support reproducible breast cancer spheroid growth, preserve epithelial architecture, and improve the reliability of hypoxia, estrogen, and drug-response assays.

03

Programmable DNA nanostructures

DNA origami-based molecular scaffolds that organize signaling proteins with nanoscale precision to reconstruct higher-order biological complexes and direct intracellular signaling.
